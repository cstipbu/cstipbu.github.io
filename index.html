About Us

Our Mission Statement

We are a group of Computer Science students who are determined to help other CS students land their dream internships and jobs. Technical interviews are a popular and important aspect of CS interviews, where an employer tests your critical thinking and problem-solving skills. We help students sharpen their technical interview skills by carefully selecting coding problems used by big tech companies in their technical interviews, and solving them in a friendly and encouraging environment. We find the patterns that connect these problems and teach you how to find them too. These guided workshops meet on Tuesday and Sunday. We are open to programmers of all ages and all technical backgrounds.



To provide flexibility for our club members, the Tuesday and Sunday meetings cover the same material. We do this so members can pick the day that best fits their schedule. Additionally, attendance is not required so members can freely choose between the Tuedsay and Sunday sessions. Each week the material is changed to ensure our members are learning from a wide range of topics. Meetings usually last one hour.
